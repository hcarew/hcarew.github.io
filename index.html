HCARE 2026

Human-Centered AI for Requirements Engineering

Montreal, Canada

The Human-Centered AI for Requirements Engineering (HCARE 2026) Workshop will be co-located with the International Conference on Requirements Engineering (RE-2026). The workshop explores how artificial intelligence, particularly large language models and generative AI, can support requirements engineers in elicitation, analysis, prioritization, and decision-making while preserving human judgment, stakeholder empathy, and responsible oversight.
